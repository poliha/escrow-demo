Two-Party Escrow
Intro Demo


The Scenario:

A car dealer sells Tunde a car for 2m NGN. Tunde does not trust the car dealer so he decides to pay the car dealer 2 days after purchasing the car, if there are no issues.

To solve this issue, Tunde and the car dealer set up an escrow that ensures:

  • Funds will only be released to the car dealer after 2 days
  • Tunde can get his money back on the 3rd day if there are any issues with the car

These can be achieved through a smart contract. Let's walk through the parameters and steps below.
